South Korea has a hierarchical system. While modernized and not taken seriously among friends, these hierarchial system lies deeply rooted in some older-fashioned workplaces. The younger, less higher up in position a worker is, the more subjected they are to "gapjil." Workplace harassment is a problem in South Korea that is not talked about enough due to many people's view it being part of a hierarchical culture.
